Hi! ☻ I'm Clara. I've been working as a front-end developer for over five years developing digital projects. I also love working in teams, and in 2018 I co-founded Desmusea, a digital cultural mediation collective. We work with technology to connect cultural institutions and citizens. I'm also part of Archivo Orsini, an online research space on philosophy, architecture, urbanism, and contemporary art.

I work with React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js and, less frequently, I also tinker with hardware technologies (Arduino/C++). I value clean code, testing, and scalability in all my projects.

Co-founder of Desmusea
2018 - present

Digital cultural mediation art collective together with Daniel Pecharromán.

Mediating means creating conditions for encounter, trying to activate bonds that make dialogue possible and promoting alternative forms of relationship and reflection. In a context of structural precarity, political and institutional disaffection, critically attending to technical modes of doing and thinking becomes urgent. We understand cultural institutions as an essential space for thinking about contemporary technologies.

We have worked with institutions such as Museo Reina Sofía, Museo Nacional del Prado, Caixafórum, Museo Nacional de Artes Decorativas, La Casa Encendida, Matadero Madrid, Museo Helga de Alvear, and Centro Cultural de España in Montevideo, and given talks at spaces like Artium, Tabakalera, and Medialab Prado.

We have also been residents at Matadero Madrid (2019-21), Quinta del Sordo (2024), and Centro Huarte (2024).
